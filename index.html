About Me

Hello! I'm Tasrina Hussan Urmi

I'm currently pursuing a B.Sc. in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering at Chittagong University of Engineering and Technology (CUET), currently in my Level 3, Term 2. My academic journey has evolved into a deep passion for Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and AI Research.

With strong proficiency in Python, C, C++, and MATLAB, I'm actively exploring neural networks, computer vision, and AI algorithms. My research interests focus on developing intelligent systems that can learn, adapt, and solve complex real-world problems.

Beyond coursework, I'm diving into research papers, implementing ML models, and building projects that combine my electronics background with AI capabilities. I'm particularly interested in how ML/DL can revolutionize telecommunication systems and create smarter, more efficient technologies.

I'm actively seeking research opportunities and collaborations in ML/DL domains, aiming to contribute to innovative projects that push the boundaries of what's possible with artificial intelligence.
